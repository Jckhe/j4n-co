Super Salon

Art & Design Specialty e-commerce at supersalon.org
WHAT IT IS
Super Salon is an art book store in Warsaw, specializing in art, photography, design and other artsy things. supersalon.org is the store's online presence where you can browse products and buy stuff.
WHAT I DID
I designed and developed the site.
HOW I DID IT
supersalon.org is fully custom e-commerce site with integrated regional payment gateways. It's based on Spree, an open-source Ruby on Rails e-commerce platform.
