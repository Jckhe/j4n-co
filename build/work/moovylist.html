Moovylist

what is it

Moovylist.com is your movie collection, online. It's a catalogue of all the films you own that you can easily share with friends.

what I Did

I designed and built it. It's a Sinatra app with MongoDB storage. The Ruby part functions mostly as an API since most of business logic, including template rendering, happens through javascript on the client-side.
